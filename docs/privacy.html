Fruit Merge Privacy Policy

Effective July 22, 2026

Fruit Merge is an offline puzzle game published by Ebulo. It does not require an account and does not ask for your name, phone number, contacts, precise location, photos, or files.

Information stored on your device

Your best score, unlocked level, coin balance, daily reward progress, and sound and vibration preferences are stored locally on your device. This information is used only to preserve game progress and settings. You can remove it by clearing the app data or uninstalling the game.

Advertising and third-party data

Fruit Merge uses Google Mobile Ads to display banner and interstitial advertisements. Depending on your device, region, and privacy choices, Google may process IP-derived approximate location, app interactions, diagnostic information, advertising identifiers, and other device or account identifiers for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention. Data transmitted by Google Mobile Ads is encrypted in transit.

Learn more in Google's Privacy Policy and Google Mobile Ads data disclosure.

Consent and privacy choices

Where required, Fruit Merge asks for advertising consent before requesting ads. Available advertising privacy choices can be reopened from the game Settings screen. You can also reset or delete your Android advertising ID from your device settings.

Children

Fruit Merge is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. The publisher configures the app's Google Play target audience and advertising settings to match its intended audience.

Retention and deletion

Game progress remains locally on your device until you clear app data or uninstall Fruit Merge. Data handled by Google is retained according to Google's policies and your privacy choices.
